Information Technology - Department

Providing innovative education since 1991

Vision of the Department

The department will strive to develop technically proficient and ethically sound diploma IT engineers to serve the industry and society.

Mission of the Department

  • M1: To impart IT engineering education through effective teaching-learning processes.
  • M2: Making our students contributory and believing in ethical values.
  • M3: Making students aware and practice current technology in the industry.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • PEO1: To be a successful IT professional with ethical values and communication skills to work as an individual and team member.
  • PEO2: To become an entrepreneur in the field of software development.
  • PEO3: To adopt emerging technologies to solve standard societal and environmental issues by lifelong learning.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • To develop Web and Mobile applications using IT Tools.
  • To understand the functionality of Computer Software and Network Systems.
